# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Primary: a solo developer or small technical team who wants a fast, private-feeling markdown workspace. They are already comfortable with code, note-taking, and AI tools, and they value speed and control over heavy onboarding. They use the app to capture ideas, organize reference material, and refine writing with an AI assistant that knows the current document.

## Product Purpose

A local-first-feeling markdown vault backed by the user’s own S3 bucket. It makes note capture, search, and AI-assisted refinement feel immediate, without the friction of a traditional CMS or the clutter of all-in-one productivity tools.

## Positioning

A calm, developer-shaped notes workspace that stays out of the way until you need it.

## Brand Personality

Calm, focused, trustworthy.

- Calm: no loud gradients, no celebratory animations, no upsell noise.
- Focused: every surface supports the task at hand; chrome recedes when not needed.
- Trustworthy: predictable behavior, clear save/etag states, honest error messages, and data the user owns.

## Anti-references

- Cluttered note apps: dense toolbars, floating widgets, competing sidebars, and visual hierarchy that fights for attention.
- Over-animated SaaS dashboards: orchestrated page-load choreography, bouncing entrance transitions, and motion that delays the first useful action.

## Design Principles

1. **The tool disappears into the task.** The UI should feel like a quiet desk, not a product tour.
2. **Density is a feature, but noise is a bug.** Information can be compact; decoration cannot be.
3. **State is visible and honest.** Saving, dirty, conflict, and error states are clear at a glance.
4. **AI is an assistant, not a host.** AI panels and suggestions are available but never dominate the workspace.
5. **Respect the user’s platform.** Light and dark modes should both feel native to the OS, not like a themed skin.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target WCAG 2.2 AA as the baseline.
- Honor `prefers-reduced-motion` by collapsing motion to instant or near-instant transitions.
- Maintain color-agnostic state cues (icons, labels, shape) alongside color.
